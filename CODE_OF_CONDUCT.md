# Code Of Conduct

AudioPilot is a small project, and the community around it should feel practical, kind, and easy to join.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially in bug reports and review feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and user impact.
- Welcome beginners and people testing on different macOS setups.
- Explain tradeoffs clearly when disagreeing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Dismissing privacy or permission concerns without engaging with the details.
- Repeatedly pushing unrelated features or promotional material.
- Sharing private security details before maintainers have had a reasonable chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable actions to keep the project healthy.

If something feels off, contact the maintainers privately when possible.
